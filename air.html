Air Freight

Uver Express Air Freight offers fast and efficient transportation of goods via air. It caters to businesses and individuals needing rapid delivery of products across long distances. By using commercial and cargo airlines, Uver Express ensures that items reach their destination quickly and securely, offering tracking services for transparency. This air freight service is ideal for time-sensitive shipments, large or small, and can handle a variety of cargo types, from perishable goods to electronics or documents.

Fast and Versatile Solutions for All Shipments

Uver's Air Freight Department offers a range of commercial air freight services for shipments of varying sizes. Additionally, we provide expedited sample courier services for urgent deliveries. In collaboration with Uver's Ocean Freight Department, we also offer a cost-effective sea-air service, ensuring fast delivery while maximizing cost savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your shipment using our tracking tool on this website: fxportal.uverexpress.com

Weight limits vary depending on the airline and aircraft type. Uver Express, for example, offers air freight services for shipments of 100kg, 500kg, 1000kg, and 2000kg, with options for larger or specialized shipments as needed.

Air freight charges are typically based on the weight and volume of the shipment. The cost may also depend on the distance, the type of service (e.g., express or standard), and any additional handling or documentation requirements.

Common documents for air freight include the air waybill, commercial invoice, packing list, export declaration, and any specific documentation related to customs clearance or restricted goods.

Air freight is ideal for time-sensitive shipments that need to reach their destination quickly, while sea freight is better suited for larger, non-urgent shipments. Uver Express offers a sea-air service for cost-effective solutions combining both methods.
