Customs Brokerage

Uver Express offers comprehensive customs brokerage services designed to streamline the import and export process. Our experienced team ensures that your shipments comply with all applicable laws and regulations, facilitating smooth customs clearance for both international and domestic shipments. We provide a wide range of services to help businesses navigate complex customs procedures, reduce delays, and avoid costly fines.

Expert Customs Brokerage and Support for International Trade

Uver's in-house licensed customs broker provides professional customs clearance services, staying up-to-date with the frequently changing customs regulations. We offer free consultations for first-time importers and exporters to help guide them through the process. In cooperation with our agents at the point of origin, Uver also offers EXW (Ex-Works) services, including the procurement of export licenses at the origin for seamless international transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

EXW (Ex-Works) refers to a shipping arrangement where the seller is responsible for making the goods available for pickup at their premises or another agreed location. Uver Express offers EXW services, including assisting with the procurement of export licenses at the origin.

While not all shipments require a customs broker, it's essential for international shipments, especially when dealing with high-value goods, sensitive products, or complex customs regulations. A customs broker can ensure compliance and smooth clearance, reducing the risk of delays.

Required documentation typically includes the commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, export license (if applicable), and any other documents specific to the goods being shipped. Uver Express ensures all necessary paperwork is prepared and submitted correctly.
